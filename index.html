About Remarkbox

Russell Ballestrini - Founder

My vision of Remarkbox started back in 2013 while porting my blog from Wordpress to Pelican, a static-site-generator.
I had a problem. What would I do with my readers comments?

My research found solutions which served ads and I didn't want that.
So I decided to build my own, because "how long could it possibly take to build an embedable comment system?" (hint: 3 years)

I put a lot of love and thought into Remarkbox. I hope you and your readers enjoy using it.
